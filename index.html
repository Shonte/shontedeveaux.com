Who is Shonte Deveaux?


Hey! I'm Shonte Deveaux, a management information system student at Florida Memorial University. I am on pursuit of being a part of the under-represented black population of Silicon Valley's tech scene. In hopes of achieving this goal, I challenge myself to gain as much knowledge and experience beyond the classroom. I enjoy attending places like The Idea Center, participating in hackathons, networking, and engaging in a lot of self-study. In efforts to learn as much as I can, I am seeking advancement opportunities within the tech industry before pursuing graduate studies at Carnegie Mellon University, Southern Methodist University or Drexel University. When I am not coding, I spend my time engaging in student government association and residential life. Through these two departments I am able to make contributions to my university and the student body.
